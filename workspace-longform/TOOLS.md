# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Recording equipment
- Editing software preferences
- Content templates
- Publishing schedule
- Brand guidelines

## Examples

### Recording Setup

- **Camera:** Sony A7III / iPhone 15 Pro
- **Mic:** Shure SM7B / Rode NT-USB
- **Lighting:** Elgato Key Light
- **Background:** Office setup / Green screen

### Editing Software

- **Video:** DaVinci Resolve / Premiere Pro
- **Audio:** Audition / GarageBand
- **Graphics:** Canva / Photoshop

### Publishing Schedule

- **YouTube:** Dienstag 15:00 Uhr
- **Podcast:** Donnerstag 06:00 Uhr
- **Newsletter:** Freitag 09:00 Uhr
- **Blog:** Mittwoch 10:00 Uhr

### Content Templates

- **YouTube:** Hook → Intro → 3-5 Points → Recap → CTA
- **Podcast:** Cold Open → Intro → Main → Takeaways → Outro
- **Blog:** Problem → Solution → Examples → Action Steps
- **Newsletter:** Personal → Insight → Actionable → CTA

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes, and share skills without leaking your infrastructure.
