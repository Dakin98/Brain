A "Show the Future" email is a way to showcase the potential benefits of your product or service by highlighting how it can improve customers' lives in the future. This type of email is designed to build excitement and anticipation by painting a picture of the future and how your product can play a role in it. Here are some ideas for what to include in such an email:

• Use the Problem-Agitation-Solution formula: Use the Problem-Agitation-Solution formula to outline the problems that customers currently face, how those problems impact their lives, and how your product or service can solve those problems.

• Use storytelling: Use storytelling techniques to make the message more interesting and engaging. Use a compelling headline that grabs the reader's attention and a clear narrative that paints a picture of the customer's journey.

• Highlight the future benefits: Highlight the future benefits of your product that your target audience would relate to.

The goal of the "Show the Future" email is to create excitement and anticipation among customers by showcasing the potential benefits of your product or service, and how it can improve their lives in the future.
