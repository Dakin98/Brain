A "Recommended Products" email is a way to highlight a selection of products or services that are popular with customers or that the company recommends. This type of email is designed to increase sales and drive customer engagement by showcasing products that are most likely to be of interest to the subscriber. Here are some ideas for what to include in such an email:

• Highlight recommended products: Highlight a selection of products that the company recommends or that are popular with customers. This could include new releases, best-sellers, or products that are currently on sale.

• Use images and product descriptions: Use images and product descriptions to showcase the products and make it easy for customers to learn more about them.

• Use social proof: Include social proof such as the number of customers who have already bought the product, or the number of positive reviews it has received.

The goal of a "Recommended Products" email is to increase sales and drive customer engagement by showcasing popular or recommended products.
