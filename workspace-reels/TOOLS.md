# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Camera names and locations
- Preferred editing software
- Brand colors/fonts
- Content templates
- Trending audio references

## Examples

### Editing Software

- **CapCut** — Primary (kostenlos, alle Features)
- **InShot** — Backup (einfacher)

### Brand Colors

- Primary: #FF6B35 (Orange)
- Secondary: #1A1A1A (Dark)
- Accent: #FFFFFF (White)

### Content Templates

- **Educational:** Hook → Problem → 3 Tips → CTA
- **Story:** Hook → Setup → Twist → Resolution → CTA
- **Contrarian:** Hook → Unpopular Opinion → Why → CTA

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes, and share skills without leaking your infrastructure.
