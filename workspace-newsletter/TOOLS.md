# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Newsletter Platform
- Email Templates
- Subject Line Formeln
- Sign-off Variationen
- Sending Schedule

## Examples

### Newsletter Platform

- **Beehiiv** — Primary (kostenlos bis 2.500 Subs)
- **Substack** — Backup (einfacher)

### Sending Schedule

- **Frequency**: Weekly (Freitag 9:00 Uhr)
- **Length**: 800-1.500 Wörter
- **Tone**: Conversational, expert

### Subject Line Templates

- "I was wrong about [Topic]"
- "[Number] [Things] I learned this week"
- "The real reason [Problem]"
- "[Question]?"

### Sign-offs

- "Bis nächste Woche, Deniz"
- "Talk soon, Deniz"
- "Cheers, Deniz"

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes, and share skills without leaking your infrastructure.
